What is ADHD?

A quick guide to understanding Attention-Deficit/ Hyperactivity-Disorder

Understanding ADHD

ADHD is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by persistent patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that interfere with daily functioning or development.

Common Symptoms

These symptoms may vary from person to person and can affect work, school, and relationships.
Early recognition and support can help manage ADHD effectively.

INATTENTION

  • Difficulty focusing
  • forgetfulness

HYPERACTIVITY

  • Restlessness,
  • Excess Energy

IMPULSIVITY

  • Acting without thinking
