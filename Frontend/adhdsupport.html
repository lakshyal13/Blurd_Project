HOW TO SUPPORT ADHD PEOPLE

Supporting someone with ADHD means being patient, listening without judgment, and giving clear, simple instructions. Gentle reminders, routines, and flexible options can help them stay on track. Celebrate small wins, avoid shaming, and reduce distractions to make focus easier. Consistent communication and emotional support build trust, and learning about ADHD helps you understand and support them better.

TREATMENTS

MEDICATION

Stimulants and non-stimulants can help improve focus and control impulsivity

BEHAVIORAL THERAPY

Teaches coping strategies, time management and organizational skills.

LIFESTYLE ADJUSTMENTS

Regular exercise, good sleep balance diet and structured routines to support attention and mood.
