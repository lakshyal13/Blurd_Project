What is DYSLEXIA?

A quick guide to understanding DYSLEXIA

Understanding Dyslexia

DYSLEXIA is a neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by persistent patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that interfere with daily functioning or development.

Common Symptoms

These symptoms may vary from person to person and can affect work, school, and relationships. Early recognition and support can help manage DYSLEXIA effectively.

Reading Difficulties

Slow, inaccurate or effortful reading.

Writing Issues

Letter reversal and poor spelling.

Processing Problems

Trouble matching sounds to letters.
